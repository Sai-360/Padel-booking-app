Padel Booking App

Book your padel court easily

Create private or public matches, join available public games, pay your participation and follow your reservations from one place.

60€ fixed match price
4 players per match
1h30 match duration

How it works

1

Choose a site

Select Brussels or Namur and choose an available court.

2

Create or join

Create a private/public match or join an existing public one.

3

Pay and play

Each player pays 15€. The match price is fixed at 60€.

Closed days

Global member up to 21 days before
Site member up to 14 days before, only on own site
Free member up to 5 days before
Unpaid balance reservation blocked until payment
